About Pakistan

A nation of incredible diversity — geographically, culturally, and historically.

Pakistan, a populous multiethnic country of South Asia, has historically and culturally been associated with its neighbours Iran, Afghanistan, and India. With a predominantly Indo-Iranian speaking population and an overwhelming Muslim majority, Pakistan has a unique identity shaped by millennia of civilisation.

Its capital is Islamabad, nestled in the foothills of the Himalayas, while its largest city is Karachi on the coast of the Arabian Sea. The country encompasses everything from soaring mountain peaks like K2 to vast fertile plains and ancient historical sites.
